PORTFOLIO



The Library of Knowledge

An INKLE Interactive Story

Speak with Shaanxi, the omniscient spirit of knowledge who offers three tales - two short stories which describe the political and geographical regions of the east and west lands of Elandris, as well as a novella explaining the protagonist's backstory. While each tale is self-contained, the choices the player makes when conversing with the spirit, and the spirit's mysterious assistant, offers an interweaving, branching story with two alternative endings.
