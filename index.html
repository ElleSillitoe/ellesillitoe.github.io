The Library of Knowledge

An INKLE Interactive Story [2 hours+]

Peruse an ancient library in which all learnt things reside, including Shaanxi, the omniscient spirit of knowledge. Shaanxi offers you three tales, one of which is yours. Discover where you came from, why you came, and why the world will never be the same after you leave. This story contains mystical beasts, menacing pirates, a journey across the world, and one lovely little goat.

Skyrider

An INKLE Interactive Story [5 mins]

A short sci-fi story set on the barren desert planet of Sikorak, a world which knows only death, dust, and desperation. Explore the harsh Vormal Desert, scavenge for supplies, and discuss religion with your mother. Inspired by Starfield.

Minecraft Frontiers

Director of Lore (2019-2021)

Managed a small team of writers to design, direct and implement games writing and narrative design for Minecraft Frontiers, a modded ancient Rome-inspired fantasy server with over 1600 registered users. Curated and maintained the Wikipedia pages covering world lore. Worked closely with coding team to script overarching narratives, factions, quest lines, dialogue, and hugely successful server events which boosted player engagement by 300%.
